Services

Odoo ERP Services

Analysing & Planning

Specify the requirements from your new ERP system. Prepare your business and plan the implementation strategy around technology, people and processes.

  • Specify the issues and challenges that your business is facing and set a plan on how you can overcome it with the help of the new system.
  • Analyze how the new system can help your business achieve short-term and long-term goals.
  • Identify which software should be integrated, upgraded, or replaced with the new system.

Implementation

In the Odoo environment, we rely on the latest technologies and work closely with the Odoo SA development team. In this way, we can ensure that requirements are mapped according to the latest standards.

The development of customer-specific apps can be carried out as part of a complete Odoo implementation project. There is also the option of having us develop customer-specific, independent apps.

Integration

Integrate value-added technology, communicate and exchange information with third parties through APIs. Power your software through integration using web services.

  • Identify the best and appropriate API solutions that respond to your business needs.
  • Integrate with other business software solutions and third-party programs and make your operations adaptable and resilient.
  • Conduct manual and automated testing after deploying your web services API integration solutions.

Migration

When migrating an Odoo system, a distinction must be made between migrating the data (Postgres database) and migrating the adjustments (Python code). Both processes take place independently of each other.

We facilitate seamless data processing and extraction for your business. Migrate to Odoo with a hassle-free approach!

Training

Behind every successful business software implementation is the organization’s workforce. Employees should be well-trained and should become experts in using the new system to deliver better value for the business.

Ever Business Solutions provides efficient and superior training services to your organization to avoid the risks of delayed adoption and implementation, errors and system failures. Training will cover all the aspects of the system’s features, highlighting business processes and how to perform daily tasks within the system.

We help your team become confident and self-sufficient users of your new business software solution.

Support

When it comes to supporting your Odoo system, trust us as an experienced and reliable partner. As Cyprus Technologies, we aim to enter into long-term partnerships with our customers. In order to support our customers holistically, we offer you professional support services.
